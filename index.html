14-Day Meal Planner

Day 1

Breakfast: Chilled oats with chia seeds, almond milk, and fresh mango slices.
Mid-Morning Snack: Coconut water & soaked almonds.
Lunch: Turai (ridge gourd) dal with brown rice, cucumber raita.
Evening Snack: Watermelon cubes with mint.
Dinner: Stir-fried paneer with sautéed parwal (pointed gourd) & whole wheat roti.

Day 2

Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with muskmelon & nuts.
Mid-Morning Snack: Buttermilk with roasted cumin.
Lunch: Quinoa with brinjal sabzi & a bowl of curd.
Evening Snack: Aam panna & roasted makhana.
Dinner: Stuffed capsicum with paneer and vegetable curry.

Day 3

Breakfast: Moong dal chilla with mint chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: Roasted peanuts & lemon water.
Lunch: Mango dal with red rice, cucumber salad.
Evening Snack: Cucumber sticks with homemade hummus.
Dinner: Bhindi (okra) stir-fry with jowar roti & salad.

Day 4

Breakfast: Masala buttermilk & fruit salad (watermelon, papaya, pomegranate).
Mid-Morning Snack: Pumpkin seeds & coconut water.
Lunch: Curd rice (brown rice) with mustard seed tempering.
Evening Snack: Chilled tomato gazpacho soup.
Dinner: Grilled tofu with sautéed zucchini & salad.

Day 5

Breakfast: Ragi porridge with banana slices & nuts.
Mid-Morning Snack: Soaked walnuts & lemon water.
Lunch: Masoor dal with parwal sabzi & bajra roti.
Evening Snack: Chilled watermelon smoothie.
Dinner: Drumstick (Sahjan) sabzi with quinoa & salad.

Day 6

Breakfast: Besan chilla with mint chutney & aam panna.
Mid-Morning Snack: Cucumber sticks with peanut butter.
Lunch: Spinach dal with jowar roti & curd.
Evening Snack: Muskmelon juice with basil seeds.
Dinner: Tofu & mixed vegetable stir-fry with whole wheat chapati.

Day 7

Breakfast: Cold sprout salad with lemon juice & herbal tea.
Mid-Morning Snack: Roasted makhana & coconut water.
Lunch: Chana dal with methi (fenugreek) sabzi & brown rice.
Evening Snack: Buttermilk with cumin & almonds.
Dinner: Paneer tikka with raw mango salad.

Day 8

Breakfast: Overnight soaked oats with yogurt & flaxseeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: Green tea with a handful of peanuts.
Lunch: Lemon quinoa with palak dal & a side of cucumber salad.
Evening Snack: Herbal tea with fox nuts.
Dinner: Mixed vegetable stew with jowar roti.

Day 9

Breakfast: Cucumber-mint smoothie & a handful of walnuts.
Mid-Morning Snack: Aam panna & roasted chana.
Lunch: Mango dal with brown rice & salad.
Evening Snack: Curd with chia seeds.
Dinner: Stir-fried karela (bitter gourd) with paneer & roti.

Day 10

Breakfast: Ragi dosa with coconut chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: Buttermilk with mint leaves.
Lunch: Capsicum sabzi with moong dal & quinoa.
Evening Snack: Fresh coconut water & roasted peanuts.
Dinner: Grilled vegetables with tofu & salad.

Day 11

Breakfast: Watermelon juice & a handful of seeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: Roasted makhana.
Lunch: Spinach & lentil soup with jowar roti.
Evening Snack: Green tea & cucumber salad.
Dinner: Paneer stir-fry with sautéed bell peppers.

Day 12

Breakfast: Besan cheela with mint chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: Lemon water & roasted chana.
Lunch: Brown rice with methi dal & a bowl of curd.
Evening Snack: Muskmelon juice with chia seeds.
Dinner: Stuffed tinda with paneer & salad.

Day 13

Breakfast: Chilled fruit salad with almonds.
Mid-Morning Snack: Aam panna & pumpkin seeds.
Lunch: Lemon quinoa with brinjal sabzi & curd.
Evening Snack: Green tea with roasted fox nuts.
Dinner: Bhindi (okra) sabzi with chapati.

Day 14

Breakfast: Ragi porridge with banana slices & flaxseeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: Fresh coconut water & walnuts.
Lunch: Chana dal with capsicum sabzi & brown rice.
Evening Snack: Buttermilk with cumin.
Dinner: Palak paneer with jowar roti.
