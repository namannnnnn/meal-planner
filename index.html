14-Day Meal Planner

Day 1

Breakfast: Vegetable dalia with a sprinkle of seeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: A handful of almonds and green tea.
Lunch: Tofu stir-fry with quinoa and sautéed vegetables.
Evening Snack: Cucumber and carrot sticks with hummus.
Dinner: Palak paneer with brown rice and mixed green salad.

Day 2

Breakfast: Oats porridge with skimmed milk and mixed seeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: A bowl of papaya or an apple.
Lunch: Moong dal khichdi with curd and steamed broccoli.
Evening Snack: Roasted chana with herbal tea.
Dinner: Grilled paneer with sautéed spinach and brown rice.

Day 3

Breakfast: Poha with peanuts and a boiled egg.
Mid-Morning Snack: A fruit of your choice.
Lunch: Rajma with brown rice and side salad.
Evening Snack: Buttermilk with roasted seeds.
Dinner: Tofu stir-fry with mixed vegetables.

Day 4

Breakfast: Besan chilla with mint chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: Yogurt with flaxseeds.
Lunch: Grilled paneer tikka with salad and whole wheat roti.
Evening Snack: Fresh coconut water with walnuts.
Dinner: Mixed lentil soup with sautéed vegetables.

Day 5

Breakfast: Scrambled paneer with whole wheat toast.
Mid-Morning Snack: A pear or orange.
Lunch: Masoor dal with quinoa and side salad.
Evening Snack: Green tea with mixed nuts.
Dinner: Grilled tofu with roasted sweet potatoes.

Day 6

Breakfast: Methi thepla with curd.
Mid-Morning Snack: Fresh fruit smoothie with no added sugar.
Lunch: Paneer curry with brown rice and steamed vegetables.
Evening Snack: Roasted makhana with herbal tea.
Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with bell peppers and mushrooms.

Day 7

Breakfast: Vegetable upma made with rava or broken wheat.
Mid-Morning Snack: Mixed berries or an apple.
Lunch: Chole with whole wheat roti and salad.
Evening Snack: Yogurt with chia seeds.
Dinner: Grilled paneer with quinoa and sautéed greens.

Day 8

Breakfast: Oats chilla with green chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: A handful of pistachios.
Lunch: Spinach and lentil stew with brown rice.
Evening Snack: Buttermilk with roasted seeds.
Dinner: Grilled tofu with steamed vegetables.

Day 9

Breakfast: Multigrain toast with avocado and boiled eggs.
Mid-Morning Snack: A bowl of pomegranate.
Lunch: Mixed vegetable sabzi with quinoa.
Evening Snack: Green tea with mixed nuts.
Dinner: Paneer bhurji with side salad.

Day 10

Breakfast: Ragi porridge with nuts.
Mid-Morning Snack: A fruit of your choice.
Lunch: Tandoori paneer with sautéed vegetables.
Evening Snack: Roasted chickpeas with herbal tea.
Dinner: Moong dal soup with brown rice.

Day 11

Breakfast: Vegetable uttapam with coconut chutney.
Mid-Morning Snack: A handful of almonds.
Lunch: Palak and moong dal with whole wheat roti.
Evening Snack: Fresh coconut water with walnuts.
Dinner: Grilled tofu with steamed vegetables.

Day 12

Breakfast: Masala paneer bhurji with whole wheat toast.
Mid-Morning Snack: Yogurt with flaxseeds.
Lunch: Rajma with quinoa and side salad.
Evening Snack: Buttermilk with roasted seeds.
Dinner: Grilled tofu with roasted sweet potatoes.

Day 13

Breakfast: Poha with peanuts.
Mid-Morning Snack: A fruit of your choice.
Lunch: Chole with whole wheat roti and salad.
Evening Snack: Green tea with mixed nuts.
Dinner: Stir-fried tofu or paneer with bell peppers and mushrooms.

Day 14

Breakfast: Methi paratha with curd.
Mid-Morning Snack: Fresh fruit smoothie with no added sugar.
Lunch: Paneer curry with brown rice and steamed vegetables.
Evening Snack: Roasted makhana with herbal tea.
Dinner: Mixed lentil soup with sautéed vegetables.
